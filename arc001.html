ARC001

ARC001 is our first edition light, shaped from gently curved oak or ash and designed to bring a calm, natural glow to any space. It's fully portable, rechargeable via USB-C, and easy to move wherever you want light to feel a little softer.

Each ARC001 is made slowly and sustainably using locally sourced wood — handcrafted in our home studio in Newcastle upon Tyne.
