Bow — a design studio based in Newcastle upon Tyne, England, owned and operated by Chris England.

Chris's work draws from a long-standing interest in craft, material, and the quiet presence of well-made objects.

Each piece is the result of finding a balance between function and form — understanding the properties of the material, and allowing those properties to shape the design.

Each Bow piece is designed and made by hand in Chris's studio in Newcastle upon Tyne.
