Background

Bow is a design studio based in Newcastle upon Tyne, England, led by Chris England.

Each piece is made by hand in Chris's home workshop, using locally sourced hardwoods selected for their grain, character, and warmth.

Chris's work is rooted in an appreciation for material honesty — the idea that a well-made object should feel like it could only have been made one way. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Bow's debut work, ARC001, is a portable lamp shaped from a single length of oak or ash. Rechargeable, minimal, and made slowly — it is designed to bring a calm, natural glow to any space.

All Bow pieces are available for commission.
